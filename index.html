Convert your text painlessly.

This tool converts the case of any text with a simple click.

Why This Tool?

All manipulation to text is done within your browser, and no text is sent to our servers. Revision histories are cleared the moment you reload this page.

 Free. Lightweight. Quick. Works directly within the browser.
 Helpful for copywriters, designers, website developers and digital marketers.
 Remove any existing styling from your current text.
 Switch up the sentence casing without worrying about privacy.
 Revision history created for each time you refresh this window.
 If you install this site on your homescreen, it'll even work offline!
 Open source. View code on Github.
